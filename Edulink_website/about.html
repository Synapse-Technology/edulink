Our Background

Edulink KE was an initiative of the Synapse JKUAT group with the support of Jhub Africa. The Synapse group is a group made up of seven Computer Technology students from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) who are passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between education and employment in Kenya.

Recognizing the challenges faced by students in securing internships and job placements, we set out to create a platform that would not only connect students with opportunities but also provide educational institutions and employers with tools to track and support talent development.

With the support of Jhub Africa, we developed Edulink KE as a comprehensive solution that integrates internship listings, digital certifications, and performance tracking. Our platform aims to empower students by providing them with the resources they need to succeed in their careers while also offering institutions and employers a reliable system to monitor and nurture talent.

Today, Edulink KE stands as a testament to our commitment to transforming the educational landscape in Kenya. We are dedicated to creating a seamless experience for students, institutions, and employers.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan students by providing seamless access to internships, job placements, and mentorship, while offering institutions and employers a reliable system to track and support talent development.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every student can easily transition from learning to working, guided by transparent systems and real-time progress tracking.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our interactions.
  • Empowerment: We believe in equipping students with the tools and opportunities they need to succeed.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with educational institutions, employers, and partners to create a supportive ecosystem for youth.
  • Innovation: We leverage technology to continuously improve our platform and services.
  • Inclusivity: We strive to ensure equal access to opportunities for all Kenyan students, regardless of their background.
  • Impact: We are committed to making a positive difference in the lives of students and the broader community.
